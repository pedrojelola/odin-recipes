Chicken breast

Delicious and easy chicken breast

You can make this convenient recipe in an oven or a pan. It only takes 5 minutes and the results are fantastic.

ingredients

  • pan or oven
  • Chicken breast
  • Oil or butter
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut the breast on small cubes
  2. Put oil or butter on your pan-oven sheet
  3. Season your breast with salt and pepper, you can add others if you like
  4. Put the chicken to cook and move it constantly
  5. When the chicken starts getting black quickly put it away from the fire
  6. Let it cool for a while and it's ready to plate
