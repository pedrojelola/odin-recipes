Avocado toast

Nutritious and quick breakfast

A quick way to eat the 3 macronutrients,fat,carbs and protein, in a quick meal. With few ingredients and a little cooking you can start your morning the rigth way

ingredients

This is the ingredients for 1 serving

  • Slice of bread
  • Half avocado
  • An egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cherry tomato (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the bread on the toaster
  2. Cook the egg. It can be fried or hard boiled
  3. Open the avocado and put half in a plate
  4. Smash the avocado until soft consistency
  5. Season the avocado with salt and pepper
  6. Assemble the recipe. First the toast, then the avocado paste and finally the egg
  7. If you feel like add a few slices of cherry tomato
