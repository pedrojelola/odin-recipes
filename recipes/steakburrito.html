Steak burrito

Recipe for the braves

Burritos are great you can fill them with anything you can imagine. This time we are using rib eye steak

ingredients

  • Rib eye steak
  • Flour tortilla
  • Salt and pepper
  • butter

Steps

  1. Put some butter in the pan and start pre heating it at medium flame
  2. Throw the steak in the pan
  3. Season it with salt and pepper
  4. Let it cook for 5 minutes then turn it
  5. When it's done both sides turn off the heat
  6. Let it rest for a minutes and the cut it
  7. Assemble your burrito and Enjoy
